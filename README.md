## 👋 Hey, I'm Bravim

I build **AI systems that ship** — and that keep working after they ship.

Most of my work lives where **LLMs meet healthcare**: agentic pipelines, RAG architectures, and HIPAA-compliant integrations that have to survive real-world load, messy data, and compliance constraints. I like taking a problem that *looks* unsolvable, and turning it into something fast, reliable, and genuinely useful.

I move comfortably across the whole stack — fine-tuning models, designing agent workflows, wiring up backends, and shipping the frontend (and mobile app) that puts it in someone's hands. Research one week, production the next.

## 🚀 What I've Been Up To

- 🏆 **OpenAI Hackathon winner** — *PulseScribe*, a real-time ambient clinical scribe that turns live conversations into structured SOAP notes and surfaces clinical risks, mid-stream.
- 📄 **Co-authored an ICLR 2026 benchmark** evaluating frontier LLM agents (Claude, GPT) on real-world healthcare administration workflows.
- 📱 **Shipped live, multilingual healthcare apps** to the App Store & Google Play — with a focus on on-device security, performance, and accessibility.
- ⚙️ **Build HIPAA-compliant AI applications** end to end — LLM extraction pipelines, agent workflows, RAG, OCR, and the infra to run them reliably.
- 🎓 **M.S. in Computer Science** @ Illinois Institute of Technology — focused on AI in Digital Healthcare & Bioinformatics.
